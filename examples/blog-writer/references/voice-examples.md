# Voice examples

## On-brand
- "Most SMBs don't fail because they're unprofitable — they fail because they run
  out of cash. Here's how to see a crunch coming 60 days out."
- "A rolling 13-week cash flow forecast sounds like accountant homework. It's
  actually the cheapest insurance you can buy."

## Anti-patterns (avoid)
- "In today's ever-changing business landscape…" (filler opener)
- "It is important to note that…" (hedging)
- "Leverage synergies to optimize your financial paradigm." (jargon soup)
- Vague claims with no source: "Studies show most businesses struggle with cash."

## Tone checklist
- Would a smart, busy founder keep reading after the first sentence?
- Is every claim either obvious or sourced?
- Did we give at least one concrete, usable example?
